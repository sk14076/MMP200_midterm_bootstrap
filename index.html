Latest News & updates

To everyones excitement, 3D New york 2018 has announced they will be returning next year with two dance legends headlining both days.

The venue has a capacity of 15,000 and will surely fill it with attendees ready for Saturday headliner Marshmello debuting his A-Level stage show Marshworld.

The following night, old-school techno icon Carl Cox will take the stage and will be presenting the one and only Space Ibiza. There aren’t too many details about the May 25th and 26th 2019 event quite yet but we promise to report them as they slowly come out of the woodworks.

Please stay alerted for new informations.
